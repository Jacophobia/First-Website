Overview

Purpose

How many tasks in your business need to be accomplished everyday that you would rather have a computer do for you? How often do you find yourself and your employees performing tedious tasks that consume a lot of company time? What you need is quality computer programs specifically designed for your companies needs. My team of software designers and developers can produce quality software that is completely customized to what you need. Once purchased, we even provide regular maintenance for a low monthly cost so our service is risk free!

Audience

This website will target business owners. I think that our main clients would be small businesses who don't have the funds to hire a full-time development team. Good software, including websites, can make a huge difference for a small company which is just getting started. I think that when they see the website, they will want our services so that they can boost their appeal to their customers. I will make the website as minimalistic and cleanly organized as possible so that it appeals to young entrepreneurs.

Content

Home page

My website for the final project will be a website for the fabricated company Durango Heights Development. The website is designed specifically to appeal to potential customers and convince them to employ the services of Durango Heights Development. Durango Heights development is a software development agency which sells customized software to a wide range of buyers, from small businesses to large corporations. These services will benefit the customer because they will not need to hire a dedicated software development team. This will save them money on hiring and it will keep their software low-maintenance and consistently up-to-date. Why will people come to my site? People will come to my site because it offers them an easy solution to one of their more complex problems. Software development is very expensive. While it can remove many different tedious or complicated tasks and mitigate errors, it is not always easy to obtain. Often people who would want this type of service simply donâ€™t have the money or time to hire their own software development team. That is why people will come to our site. They will see that there is a way for them to obtain high quality software without spending large amounts of time and money one finding and employing their own team.

Images for the Home page

[Page 2]

What questions will my visitors expect my site to answer for them? Visitors to my site will expect a few of their questions to be answered. First, they will come to the website hoping to see how employing us will be better than continuing to do things the way they do. The website will show an in-depth analysis of our service and the potential financial gain available to the company for using our services. Second, they will want to know how our services are better than services offered by competitors. To show them that we are a better option, the site will show that our software initial costs are higher, but that we have a very low maintenance cost which will lower the cost over time. Third, they will want to know if they can trust the quality of software we produce. We will then refer to the credibility of our programs and the expectation for quality that we uphold within the company. We will also give a breakdown of our developers so they can see the experience and education of each employee.

What types of activities will visitors want to complete on my site? I hope to have an interactive site that is not only informative, but also gives the customer an opportunity to get a hands-on feel for what the company is capable of and what we have to offer. One of the things the customer should be able to do on the website is get an estimate for the potential cost of a service and the time it will take for the service to be provided. I will place interactive tools that will give them a chance to see exactly what we can do for them. What information will we supply so that they can take actions that our site offers? We will provide them with at least three forms of contact that they can use if they desire our services as well as a request form that they can submit in the website to get an estimate for how much it will cost and how much time it will take for us to provide a service to them.
